# Huntington Data Lake: Tables Granularity & Content Report

This report provides a brief description of the tables generated by the data lake scripts, including their final output tables, information content, granularity, and join rules.

## 1. Clinisys & Embryoscope Combined Data

* **Script:** [01_merge_clinisys_embryoscope.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/data_lake_scripts/01_merge_clinisys_embryoscope.py)
* **Final Output Table:** `gold.embryoscope_clinisys_combined`
* **Direct Source Tables:**
  * `gold.clinisys_embrioes` (processed Clinisys embryos)
  * `gold.embryoscope_embrioes` (processed Embryoscope embryos)
* **Information Content:** Merges detailed clinical records of embryos and oocytes from Clinisys (e.g., micromanipulation, freezing, thawing, patient treatments, and doctor associations) with culture and morphokinetic tracking data from the Embryoscope API (e.g., fertilization time, well number, embryo fate, and embryo description).
* **Granularity:** **One row per embryo (or oocyte)** under micromanipulation from Clinisys (originating from `gold.clinisys_embrioes`).
* **Join Logic & Matching Rules:**
  * Uses a `LEFT JOIN` on `gold.clinisys_embrioes` to preserve all Clinisys records.
  * Joins with `gold.embryoscope_embrioes` where:
    * Exact patient chart matches: `c.micro_prontuario = e.prontuario`
    * Exact embryo number matches: `c.oocito_embryo_number = e.embryo_embryo_number`
    * Approximate date matches: `e.embryo_FertilizationTime` (as Date) falls within $\pm 3$ days of `c.micro_Data_DL`.

## 2. Redlara & Planilha Combined Data

* **Script:** [02_combine_redlara_planilha.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/data_lake_scripts/02_combine_redlara_planilha.py)
* **Final Output Table:** `gold.redlara_planilha_combined`
* **Direct Source Tables:**
  * `silver.redlara_unified` (unified Redlara patient outcomes)
  * `silver.planilha_embriologia_combined` (fresh & FET embryo worksheets)
* **Information Content:** Merges patient cycle, embryo transfer, and pregnancy/delivery outcomes from the Redlara dataset with embryo culture, transfer, and cryopreservation data from the manual embryology spreadsheets (Planilha Embriologia). Standardizes variables such as the incubator type (`incubadora_padronizada`) and number of newborns (`merged_numero_de_nascidos`).
* **Granularity:** **One row per embryo transfer event or patient cycle (FET or Fresh)** per patient prontuario.
* **Join Logic & Matching Rules:**
  * Uses a `FULL OUTER JOIN` via a prioritized 1-to-1 **Waterfall Match Strategy** to pair Redlara and Planilha cycles:
    * **Step 1:** Exact transfer date match (`r.date_of_embryo_transfer = e.fet_data_da_fet`).
    * **Step 2:** Transfer date match within a $\pm 3$-day window.
    * **Step 3:** Exact cryopreservation date (FET) match (`r.date_when_embryos_were_cryopreserved = e.fet_data_crio`).
    * **Step 4:** Cryopreservation date (FET) match within a $\pm 3$-day window.
    * **Step 5:** Exact cryopreservation date (Fresh) match (`r.date_when_embryos_were_cryopreserved = e.fresh_data_crio`).
    * **Step 6:** Cryopreservation date (Fresh) match within a $\pm 3$-day window.
  * Only records with a valid patient chart number (`prontuario > 0`) are matched.
  * Unmatched records from either dataset are preserved in the output table (with the opposite side's fields as `NULL`).

## 3. Embryoscope & Planilha Combined Data

* **Script:** [03_combine_embryoscope_planilha.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/data_lake_scripts/03_combine_embryoscope_planilha.py)
* **Final Output Table:** `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined`
* **Direct Source Tables:**
  * `gold.embryoscope_clinisys_combined` (joined Clinisys-Embryoscope embryo records)
  * `gold.redlara_planilha_combined` (joined Redlara-Planilha cycles)
* **Information Content:** Combines matched Clinisys-Embryoscope embryo-level records with Redlara-Planilha cycle-level data. It computes unified clinical status flags for each embryo, such as `is_transferred_combined` (whether the embryo was transferred), `has_biopsy` (whether PGT-A/biopsy was performed), `has_valid_outcome` (whether a valid pregnancy or clinical outcome is registered), and `has_transfer_logging_gap` (checks if a pregnancy outcome is registered but no transfer event was recorded).
* **Granularity:** **One row per embryo (or oocyte)** from Clinisys (originating from `gold.embryoscope_clinisys_combined`).
* **Join Logic & Matching Rules:**
  * Uses a `LEFT JOIN` on `gold.embryoscope_clinisys_combined` to keep all embryo-level records.
  * Links each embryo to its patient cycle/transfer in `gold.redlara_planilha_combined` using a 9-step **Waterfall Match Strategy** based on the patient chart (`prontuario`) and relevant dates:
    * **Step 1:** Match prontuario + exact punção date (`e.puncao_date = s.fresh_data_da_puncao`).
    * **Step 2:** Match prontuario + exact transfer date from thawing record (`e.transfer_date_descong = s.transfer_date`).
    * **Step 3:** Match prontuario + exact transfer date from treatment 1 record (`e.transfer_date_trat1 = s.transfer_date`).
    * **Step 4:** Match prontuario + exact transfer date from treatment 2 record (`e.transfer_date_trat2 = s.transfer_date`).
    * **Step 5:** Match prontuario + exact cryopreservation date from Redlara (`e.cong_date = s.date_when_embryos_were_cryopreserved`).
    * **Step 6:** Match prontuario + exact cryopreservation date from FET (`e.cong_date = s.fet_data_crio`).
    * **Step 7:** Match prontuario + exact cryopreservation date from Fresh (`e.cong_date = s.fresh_data_crio`).
    * **Step 8:** Match prontuario + approximate punção date ($\pm 3$ days window).
    * **Step 9:** Match prontuario + approximate transfer date ($\pm 3$ days window across thaw, treatment 1, or treatment 2 dates).
  * Grouping and prioritization filter out non-transferred matches if a transfer occurred in the cycle, resolving 1-to-many matches. Unmatched embryos keep their Redlara-Planilha cycle fields as `NULL`.

## 4. Ploidia Data Table (Initial Mapping & Post-Processing)

* **Pipeline Scripts:**
  1. **Creation/Mapping:** [01_create_data_ploidia_table.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/planilha_ploidia/01_create_data_ploidia_table.py)
  2. **Imputation (Enrichment):** [02_fill_missing_values.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/planilha_ploidia/02_fill_missing_values.py)
  3. **Image Metadata (Enrichment):** [03_join_image_availability.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/planilha_ploidia/03_join_image_availability.py)
* **Final Output Table:** `gold.data_ploidia` (Fully Enriched)
* **Direct Source Tables:**
  * `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined` (base embryo clinical record)
  * `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos` (used for weight, height, diagnosis, and previous transfer count audits)
  * `silver.embryo_image_availability_latest` (used to attach image metadata response logs)
* **Information Content:** Selected subset of columns specifically formatted for PGT-A ploidy analysis. It maps variables from `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined` to target names using the [00_02_column_mapping.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/planilha_ploidia/00_02_column_mapping.py) configuration. It contains morphokinetic timing variables (`t2` to `tSC`), visual scoring metrics (`ICM`, `TE`, fragmentation), patient demographics, biopsy statuses, and pregnancy outcomes.
  * **Calculated / Enriched fields in this pipeline:**
    * `Video ID`: Concatenation of the patient chart and embryo ID (`micro_prontuario || '_' || embryo_EmbryoID`).
    * `Oocyte Source`: Classification of egg source (`Heterólogo` for egg donation based on oocyte history, otherwise default to `Homólogo` for autologous).
    * `Previus ET` & `Previus OD ET` Fallback: Calculates cumulative previous embryo transfers (total and donor-egg transfers) for the patient using `clinisys.silver.view_tratamentos` records prior to the current cycle reference date.
    * **BMI & Diagnosis Imputation (Script 02):** Fills missing/blank `BMI` and `Diagnosis` fields by looking up the patient's (`prontuario`) most frequent height/weight and primary infertility factor (`fator_infertilidade1`) recorded in `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos`.
    * **Image Availability (Script 03):** Adds columns `api_response_code` and `api_error_message` by joining with `silver.embryo_image_availability_latest` on exact slide/embryo ID to flag if culture images were successfully retrieved from the Embryoscope API.
* **Granularity:** **One row per embryo** that has either a recorded biopsy (`has_biopsy = True`) OR a valid clinical outcome (`has_valid_outcome = True`).
* **Join Logic & Filtering Rules:**
  * Base table is `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined`.
  * Filter criteria applied:
    * `micro_prontuario` (Patient ID) is not null.
    * `embryo_EmbryoID` (Slide ID) is not null.
    * `has_biopsy = True OR has_valid_outcome = True`.
  * **Imputation Join:** Updates `gold.data_ploidia` dynamically by querying `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos` per patient ID.
  * **Image Metadata Join:** Joins `gold.data_ploidia` with `silver.embryo_image_availability_latest` where `gold.data_ploidia."Slide ID" = s.embryo_EmbryoID`.

## 5. Embryos with Prescription (Research Data Flow)

* **Pipeline Scripts:**
  1. **Long Format Join:** [01_join_prescriptions.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/embryos_with_prescription/01_join_prescriptions.py)
  2. **Wide Format Pivot:** [02_create_wide_table.py](file:///g:/My%20Drive/projetos_individuais/Huntington/embryos_with_prescription/02_create_wide_table.py)
* **Final Output Tables:**
  * `gold.embryos_with_prescription_long` (Intermediate/Long Table)
  * `gold.embryos_with_prescription_wide` (Final Research/Wide Table)
* **Direct Source Tables:**
  * For `gold.embryos_with_prescription_long`:
    * `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined` (base embryo clinical record)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_medicamentos_prescricoes` (patient medication prescriptions)
  * For `gold.embryos_with_prescription_wide`:
    * `gold.embryos_with_prescription_long` (intermediate long table)
* **Information Content:** Joins patient cycle and embryo clinical data from `gold.planilha_embryoscope_combined` with medication prescriptions from Clinisys (`clinisys_all.silver.view_medicamentos_prescricoes`). It pivots and consolidates all prescriptions horizontally so that each unique medication group (e.g., Progesterone, Estrogen, etc.) gets dedicated columns containing total dose (`_dose`), duration in days (`_days`), start/end dates (`_start`, `_end`), unit (`_unit`), and interval (`_interval`).
* **Granularities:**
  * **Long Table (`gold.embryos_with_prescription_long`):** **One row per embryo per prescription** (expanded horizontally per medication entry).
  * **Wide Table (`gold.embryos_with_prescription_wide`):** **One row per embryo** (consolidated at the oocyte/embryo level).
* **Join Logic & Reshaping Rules:**
  * Filter criteria applied to source:
    * `embryo_EmbryoID` (Slide ID) is not null.
  * **Long Join:** Joins the filtered embryos with Clinisys prescriptions (`view_medicamentos_prescricoes`) on cycle card ID: `e.trat1_id = p.ficha_id`.
  * **Wide Pivot & Assembly:**
    * Aggregates the long table per `(oocito_id, presc_grupo_medicamento)` to sum total doses/days and find start/end date ranges.
    * Reshapes using DuckDB's `PIVOT` statement on `presc_grupo_medicamento`.
    * Performs a `LEFT JOIN` linking distinct embryo clinical metadata with the pivoted prescription columns.

  * `gold.patient_info` (Table): Patient-level clinic unit and physician dimensional mapping.

  * `gold.biopsy_pgta_timeline`, `gold.comprehensive_biopsy_pgta_timeline`, & `gold.resumed_biopsy_pgta_timeline` (Tables): Monthly tracking and comparison of clinical biopsies/PGT-A procedures vs. billing invoices.

  * `gold.embryo_freeze_timeline` (Table): Monthly tracking of embryos frozen, thawed, and discarded (with data quality checks).
  * `gold.cryopreservation_events_timeline` & `gold.resumed_cryopreservation_events_timeline` (Tables): Monthly freezing events (embryos, eggs, semen) vs. actual cryopreservation billing.
  * `gold.consultas_timeline` & `gold.resumed_consultas_timeline` (Tables): Monthly clinical consultation events vs. consultation billing.
* **Direct Source Tables by Output:**
  * **For `gold.all_patients_timeline`:**
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos` (treatments)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_extrato_atendimentos_central` (appointments)

    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_congelamentos_ovulos` (egg freezing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_descongelamentos_embrioes` (embryo thawing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_descongelamentos_ovulos` (egg thawing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_unidades` (clinic units)
  * **For `gold.recent_patients_timeline`:**
    * `gold.all_patients_timeline` (base timeline)
  * **For `gold.patient_info`:**
    * `gold.all_patients_timeline` (base patients)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos` (treatments for doctor mapping)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_medicos` (doctors list)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_pacientes` (patient origin details)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_unidades` (clinic units)
  * **For `gold.finops_summary`:**
    * `gold.all_patients_timeline` (base timeline)
    * `silver.mesclada_vendas` (Protheus billing invoices)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_tratamentos` (treatments for donor/recipient flags)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_medicos` (doctors list)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_pacientes` & `clinisys_all.silver.view_unidades` (unit naming)
  * **For Biopsy/PGT-A timelines (`gold.biopsy_pgta_timeline`, `gold.comprehensive_biopsy_pgta_timeline`, & `gold.resumed_biopsy_pgta_timeline`):**
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_micromanipulacao` & `clinisys_all.silver.view_micromanipulacao_oocitos` (micromanipulation clinical procedures)
    * `silver.mesclada_vendas` (Protheus billing invoices)
    * `gold.patient_info` (clinic/doctor dimensions)
  * **For Embryoscope usage timelines (`gold.embryoscope_timeline` & `gold.resumed_embryoscope_timeline`):**
    * `gold.embryoscope_embrioes` (culture records in Embryoscope)
    * `silver.mesclada_vendas` (Protheus billing invoices)
  * **For Embryo freezing timelines (`gold.embryo_freeze_timeline`):**
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_congelamentos_embrioes` (embryo freezing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_descongelamentos_embrioes` (embryo thawing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_embrioes_congelados` (frozen embryos details)
  * **For Cryopreservation event timelines (`gold.cryopreservation_events_timeline` & `gold.resumed_cryopreservation_events_timeline`):**
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_congelamentos_embrioes` (embryo freezing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_congelamentos_ovulos` (egg freezing)
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_congelamentos_semen` (semen freezing)
    * `silver.mesclada_vendas` (Protheus billing invoices)
  * **For Consultations timelines (`gold.consultas_timeline` & `gold.resumed_consultas_timeline`):**
    * `clinisys_all.silver.view_extrato_atendimentos_central` (appointments)
    * `silver.mesclada_vendas` (Protheus billing invoices)
* **Information Content:** Builds a complete, chronological clinical history of patient events (treatments, appointments, freezes/thaws) and audits them against ERP sales records (`silver.mesclada_vendas`) to detect payment gaps, unbilled procedures, or data recording discrepancies.
* **Granularities:**
  * **Unified timelines (`all_patients_timeline`, `recent_patients_timeline`):** **One row per patient event** (treatment, thaw, freeze, or appointment).
  * **Audit summaries & dimensions (`patient_info`, `finops_summary`):** **One row per patient (prontuario)**.
  * **Domain timelines (`biopsy_pgta_timeline`, `embryoscope_timeline`, `embryo_freeze_timeline`, `cryopreservation_events_timeline`, `consultas_timeline`):** **One row per patient per calendar month (period_month)**.
  * **Resumed domain timelines (`resumed_biopsy_pgta_timeline`, `resumed_embryoscope_timeline`, `resumed_cryopreservation_events_timeline`, `resumed_consultas_timeline`):** **One row per patient (prontuario)**, showing the latest status/cumulative sums and payment balances.
* **Join Logic & Rules:**
  * Aligns multiple clinical source tables using patient ID (`prontuario`) and event date.
  * Combines clinical timelines with monthly Protheus billing records using `prontuario` and `period_month`.
  * Computes payment discrepancies, identifying missing billings or missing clinical records.
