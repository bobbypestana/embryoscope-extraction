## 4. Root Cause Analysis

1. **Athena Large Table Truncation**:
   - **`view_extrato_atendimentos_central`**: Truncated at exactly 500,000 rows in Athena Silver vs 630,369 rows locally. This indicates that the 500k limit cap is enforced during the dlt/Glue pipeline upload stage.
   - **`view_medicamentos_prescricoes`**: Athena Silver has 133,538 rows vs 196,833 locally. The Cloud pipeline has a significant truncation or ingestion gap for this view.
2. **Athena Freshness Advantage**:
   - The AWS Athena pipeline is fresher by 2 days, capturing registrations and updates through May 21, whereas the local database snapshot has not been updated since May 19.
3. **Table Exclusions**:
   - The 4 extra tables (`view_agenda`, `view_agendas`, `view_indicacao_novo`, and `view_procedimentos`) are ingested to the AWS S3 staging area but are not configured for local DuckDB processing in `clinisys/params.yml`.
